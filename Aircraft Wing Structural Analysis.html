Aircraft Wing Structural Analysis

Finite element simulation of wing stiffness and stress response under aerodynamic loads

Project Overview

  • Modeled a detailed aircraft wing assembly in Fusion 360 including spars, ribs, stringers, and skin panels.
  • Transferred geometry to FEMAP and solved with NX Nastran for realistic distributed aerodynamic loading conditions.
  • Validated mesh quality with Jacobian values above 0.6 and confirmed acceptable peak displacement near 0.15 inches.
  • Interpreted stress and displacement contours to evaluate load paths, deflection behavior, and structural efficiency.
