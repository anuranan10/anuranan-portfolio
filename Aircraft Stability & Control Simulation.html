Aircraft Stability & Control Simulation

Closed-loop flight simulation with aerodynamic derivative modeling and 6-DOF visualization

Project Overview

  • Designed a subsonic aircraft and computed aerodynamic and stability derivatives using analytical methods, stability equations, and USAF DATCOM.
  • Built a MATLAB/Simulink closed-loop flight dynamics model with lookup tables for non-linear aerodynamic behavior.
  • Integrated model outputs with FlightGear for 6-DOF motion visualization and control maneuver testing.
  • Evaluated lateral, longitudinal, and directional static stability to verify control effectiveness in pitch, roll, and yaw.
