Wind Tunnel Drag Study: Flap Deflection

Experimental wake-survey analysis of flap deflection effects on drag

Project Overview

  • Performed wake surveys on a 2D NACA 4412 wing with flap deflections from 0 degrees to 50 degrees using a pitot-static probe.
  • Processed velocity-deficit measurements and calculated drag using trapezoidal numerical integration in MATLAB.
  • Compared drag trends across flap settings and confirmed expected increase in drag with higher deflection angles.
  • Documented mounting constraints and measurement uncertainty sources to improve reliability of interpretation.
