Aerospace Engineering Projects

Hands-on experiences in flight dynamics, experimental aerodynamics, and structural simulation

High-Bypass Turbofan Engine Senior Design

    Led the design of a 74,770 lbf high-bypass separate-exhaust turbofan engine, covering the full development cycle from cycle analysis and turbomachinery sizing to combustor design and performance evaluation across takeoff, cruise, and loiter mission requirements.

DC-8 PID Controller Design

    Group project developing automated flight controllers for the McDonnell Douglas DC-8, modeling the aircraft's longitudinal and lateral-directional dynamics and tuning a PID control system to regulate angle of attack and forward speed within specified overshoot and settling time constraints.

Quanser Aero PID Control Design

    Collaborated with a three-person team to design and evaluate PID controllers for the Quanser Aero pitch control system, achieving 4.12% overshoot and 1.18s peak time against specified performance requirements.

TDRS-M Orbital Mechanics and Trajectory Design

    Group project simulating the complete trajectory and maneuver sequence of a communications satellite from Low Earth Orbit to Geostationary Earth Orbit, modeling fuel burns, propellant consumption, and a precision phasing maneuver to validate real-world mission tradeoffs against NASA's TDRS-M mission profile.

Aircraft Stability & Control Simulation

    Designed a subsonic aircraft model and computed aerodynamic derivatives to evaluate flight characteristics across control inputs. Built a closed-loop flight simulation in MATLAB/Simulink and validated pitch, roll, and yaw stability requirements, with 3D maneuver visualization in FlightGear.

Wind Tunnel Drag Study: Flap Deflection

    Executed an experimental wake survey on a NACA 4412 wing across five flap deflection angles to quantify the aerodynamic impact of high-lift devices. Processed airflow measurements using MATLAB to extract drag coefficients and validated results against theoretical predictions.

Aircraft Wing Structural Analysis

    Modeled a full aircraft wing assembly including spars, ribs, stringers, and skin in 3D CAD, then simulated structural behavior under aerodynamic loading using finite element analysis. Assessed stress distribution and deformation to confirm the wing meets structural performance requirements.

Database Application for Aircraft Fleet Selection

    Developed an interactive database application enabling airline operators to search, filter, and compare aircraft across key performance parameters. Reduced data retrieval time by 25 percent and integrated data visualizations to support procurement decisions.
