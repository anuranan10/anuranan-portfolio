Quanser Aero PID Control Design

Team-based pitch-control PID design and validation on the Quanser Aero platform

Project Overview

  • Worked in a three-person team to design and evaluate PID controllers for Quanser Aero pitch control.
  • Selected and tuned gains to satisfy transient response requirements.
  • Achieved 4.12% overshoot and 1.18 second peak time in performance validation.
  • Compared test responses to target specifications to confirm controller quality.
